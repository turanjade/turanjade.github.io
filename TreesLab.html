TreesLab Wiki

Founded in 2023, the initial members of TreesLab include Ran Tu, Qiuzi Chen, Shan Xue, and Yanfeng Xu.

"Trees" refers to Transportation, ecology, economy, and sustainability. The aim of TreesLab is to facilitate a sustainable, efficient, and eco-friendly future of the transportation sector.Now, the group is expanding, with more masters and PhD students joining in. We are always push forward towards the goal of the establishment.
